Preliminary Concepts

  1. Plastics have specific manufacturing histories subsumed by polymerization.
  2. The 3D printer engages circuits of manufacturing, and stages a return from the developing world to the tech-forward home.
  3. The 3D printer may be a vapor machine as much as a plastics machine: externalities become sensible subjects.
